Mario World Map

This project combines nostalgic aesthetics with modern cartographic tools to create a playful map styled after the iconic Super Mario series. The map was designed using Mapbox Studio, with custom stylizations mimicking the visual themes of early Mario games. The interactive website was developed using JavaScript and HTML, bringing the map to life with features that allow users to explore various geographies in vibrant, pixel-art-inspired detail. From the bold, colorful roads and landmarks to the pixelated fonts and retro design, this map pays homage to the visual storytelling of Super Mario. It showcases cities, highways, and natural features, reimagined in the style of a video game map. This project demonstrates the creative potential of combining cartographic expertise with a love of classic video games, offering an interactive, fun, and engaging way to view geography. Play around and explore the world of Super Mario through a modern mapping lens!
