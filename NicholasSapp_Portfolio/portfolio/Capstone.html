Capstone

Our capstone project focuses on designing optimal routes for the Madison Marathon and IRONMAN events, combining community engagement with advanced route planning techniques. To create paths that balance safety, accessibility, and the unique geography of Madison, we conducted interviews with event organizers, athletes, and city planners to gather valuable insights. Additionally, we distributed surveys to participants and residents to understand their preferences, concerns, and feedback on existing routes. By integrating findings from these qualitative methods with in-depth analysis of scholarly literature and case studies on endurance events, we developed recommendations for route optimization. This project highlights the importance of stakeholder collaboration and data-driven planning to create routes that enhance participant experience, minimize disruptions, and showcase Madison's scenic and urban landscapes.
